Discography

Queen released a total of 15 studio albums during their career. These albums showcase their versatility, spanning various genres from rock and pop to opera and progressive music. Their iconic releases include albums like "A Night at the Opera," "Sheer Heart Attack," "A Day at the Races," "The Game," and "News of the World." Their diverse musical styles contributed significantly to their lasting impact on the music industry.
